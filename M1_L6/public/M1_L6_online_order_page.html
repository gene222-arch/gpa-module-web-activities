Chef Paz

 Chef Bryan Emmanuelle Paz, also known as Boy Puyat or Chef Boy, is a Filipino celebrity chef known for his cooking shows, Idol sa Photoshop and Chef Boy Puyat: Kusina Master and Photoshop Master.

Chef Philip

 Chef Gene Philip Artisa a.k.a "The Source", is a British chef, restaurateur, writer, and television personality. He was born in Johnstone, Scotland, and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon, England. His restaurants have been awarded 16 Michelin stars in total and currently hold a total of seven.
